Location


Welcome to University of Ghana Computer Science Department!


The university's Computer Science Department , centrally located opposite the Mathematics Department on campus, offers a dynamic environment for students, faculty, and visitors. Positioned for easy access to industry partnerships and tech career opportunities in Accra, the campus boasts modern facilities and cutting-edge infrastructure. Equipped with state-of-the-art classrooms and collaborative spaces, the department supports hands-on learning and research. It fosters a culture of innovation, preparing students for the demands of the tech industry. The website provides comprehensive information on programs and faculty for prospective students, current community members, and visitors. The department looks forward to welcoming individuals to its vibrant community and invites them to explore exciting opportunities in computer science.
