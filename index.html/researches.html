Cutting-Edge Research in Computer Science

The Computer Science Department in University of Ghana, being the country's premier university, is committed to advancing the field of computer science through innovative research. Our faculty members are leading experts in their respective areas, and they are constantly pushing the boundaries of knowledge.


Research Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Data Science
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Computer Graphics
  • Networking and Security
  • Software Engineering

Current Research Projects

AI-Powered Healthcare

People in the deparment are constantly research ways they can develop AI-powered tools to diagnose diseases, predict patient outcomes, and personalize treatments so they can give back to society.


Big Data Analytics for Business

The computer science department is also using big data analytics to help businesses make better decisions and improve their operations.


Interactive Virtual Environments

Our researchers are creating immersive virtual environments for training, education, and entertainment.


Secure Networking Solutions

We are developing secure networking solutions to protect against cyberattacks. In today's interconnected world, cybersecurity is of paramount importance. Our researchers are dedicated to developing innovative secure networking solutions to safeguard against cyberattacks that threaten data confidentiality, integrity, and availability.


Agile Software Development

Our researchers are investigating new methods for developing software more quickly and efficiently. An example is that students and other researchers in the department make annual updates to the university's general website and other software associated with the university.
