Recommended Books


  • "Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship" by Robert C. Martin

    • In this book, Robert C. Martin (Uncle Bob) provides valuable insights into writing clean, maintainable, and efficient code. It's a must-read for software developers looking to improve their coding practices and design skills.


  • "Eloquent JavaScript" by Marijn Haverbeke

    • This book is an ideal resource for those learning JavaScript. It covers the language fundamentals and progresses to more advanced topics, providing hands-on exercises and projects. It's accessible for beginners but also delves into complex concepts, making it suitable for all skill levels.


  • "Introduction to Algorithms" by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein

    • Commonly referred to as CLRS, this book is a fundamental text on algorithms and data structures. It's widely used in computer science courses and provides a solid foundation in algorithmic principles.
